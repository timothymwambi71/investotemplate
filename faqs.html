FAQs

Get the Answers to Your Common Questions

Ensuring secure land ownership is our priority.

The process typically involves land surveying, verification of ownership, processing documents with the Ministry of Lands, Housing and Urban Development, and final registration. We guide you through each step, ensuring a transparent and efficient process to get your genuine land title.

We are committed to eco-friendly practices.

Our sustainable developments incorporate environmentally conscious building materials, energy-efficient designs, responsible waste management systems, and water conservation methods. We aim to create properties that are not only comfortable but also minimize their ecological footprint.

Yes, we serve clients across Uganda.

While our main office is in Kampala, we have active projects and provide services in various districts across Uganda, including rural and peri-urban areas. Our goal is to extend sustainable solutions and secure land ownership to as many communities as possible.

Due diligence is crucial for land transactions.

We strongly recommend performing a thorough search at the Ministry of Lands, Housing and Urban Development. We can assist you with this process, including verifying the cadastral map, checking for encumbrances, and ensuring all details match official records, giving you peace of mind.
